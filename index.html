Sometimes, you gotta grow.
The first stage of a plant's life, where a seed awakens and sprouts. With the right conditions of moisture, warmth, and light, the seedling emerges from the soil, reaching towards the sun.
As the plant grows, it develops delicate stems and leaves. This stage requires nurturing care, protection, and a steady supply of water and nutrients to strengthen its roots and support healthy growth.
The plant enters a phase of rapid growth. Lush leaves and branches expand, harnessing sunlight to fuel its growth. It requires ample sunlight, proper watering, and nutrient-rich soil.
The plant reveals its vibrant and colorful flowers or develops fruits. Flowers attract pollinators, facilitating reproduction, while fruits develop and ripen to disperse seeds for future generations.
